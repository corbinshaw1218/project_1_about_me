I am a really big wildlife person. One of my favorite animals that I have seen in the wild was a brown bear in Oregon. Brown bears have always been a favorite animal of mine, but I have always wanted to see an Alaska Peninsula brown bear. Last year I went to Alaska for a vacation and we did a lot of hiking and exploring there.

During this trip, I was really hoping to see a brown bear, not to close though. These bares are coastal bears that are possibly the biggest bears on the planet today. Other than the kodiak giant bear. These bears mainly live on a salmon diet.

In the U.S. there are approximately 32,000 brown bears and out of that, 4000 of them are coastal brown bears. These bears in specific can get up to 2.74 meters tall and around 1000 lbs if they are male. The females only get up to around 600 lbs.
